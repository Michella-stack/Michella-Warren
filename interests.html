Interests & Open Discussion

What Moves Me

The ideas, spaces, and conversations I find myself returning to — professionally and personally.

01

Data Ethics & Responsible AI

How do we build statistical models and data products that serve people fairly? I'm deeply interested in the ethics of algorithmic decision-making, especially in social contexts like healthcare, housing, and policy.

02

Population Dynamics & Social Change

Demographic transitions, migration, urbanisation — the big slow forces that quietly reshape societies. My research background keeps pulling me back to questions about how populations move, adapt, and endure.

03

Product for Social Impact

Can great product thinking be applied to social challenges? I'm fascinated by organisations building at the intersection of tech and public good — and how to bring rigour without losing humanity.

04

Storytelling with Data

Numbers without narrative are just noise. I care about making data legible — visualisation, communication, and the craft of turning a complex dataset into something someone can actually act on.

05

Sustainable Cities & Urban Futures

My thesis work on Tromsø and Oslo sparked a lasting interest in how cities navigate the tension between growth and ecological responsibility — a question that feels more urgent every year.

Let's Have a Conversation

I believe some of the best ideas happen in open, informal exchange. If any of the above resonates — or if you're working on something at the intersection of data, research, and product — I'd genuinely love to hear from you.
